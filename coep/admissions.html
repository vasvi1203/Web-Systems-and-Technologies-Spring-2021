Admissions

COEP is amongst the most competitive colleges when it comes to the admissions. Admissions are usually held in the months of June-July for graduation and under-graduation courses.

COEP being an autonomous institute of Govt. of Maharashtra , all admissions to coep are centralised and effected by the commissionerate of Admissions, Maharashtra state, based on Maharashtra state CET only.

Please log onto www.mahacet.org for more details.

About Us

College of Engineering Pune, is a college affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University in Pune, Maharashtra, India. Established in 1854, it is one of the oldest engineering colleges in India, after College of Engineering, Guindy, Chennai and IIT Roorkee.
